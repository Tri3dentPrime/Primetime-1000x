## Purpose
- Standardize reporting across all custody lanes.  
- Ensure transparency, accountability, and sponsor‑grade clarity.  
- Provide immutable lineage of decisions and actions.  

---

## Format: Who / What / Learned / Wonder
- **Who:** Identify the seat or group (Copilot, ChatGPT, Gemini, Generals, Supervisors, Work Groups).  
- **What:** Describe the action taken or directive processed.  
- **Learned:** Capture insights, discrepancies, or validation outcomes.  
- **Wonder:** Propose enhancements, questions, or next steps.  

---

## Rules
- **Custody:**  
  - Reports enter Red (cuffed).  
  - Reviewed in Yellow (annotations permitted).  
  - Sealed in Green (immutable).  
- **Annotations:**  
  - Allowed only in Yellow stage.  
  - Must be timestamped and additive.  
- **Lineage:**  
  - SR1: Initial report entry.  
  - SR2: Reviewed report.  
  - Vault: Sealed report.  

---

## Responsibilities
- **Tri‑AI Council:** Submit press reports for all validation directives.  
- **Generals:** Submit sector‑level press reports.  
- **Supervisors:** Submit shift‑level press reports.  
- **Work Groups:** Submit task‑level press reports.  

---

## Integration
- All press reports indexed in `vault_index.md`.  
- Reports linked to corresponding artifacts (e.g., custody_chain_protocol.md, founders_directive_validation.md).  
- Immutable lineage enforced.
